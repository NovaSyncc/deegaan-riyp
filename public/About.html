About Deegaan-Riyo
Deegaan-Riyo, a subsidiary of RiyO, is a premium digital hotel showcase platform featuring the Top 10 Best Hotels across major East African cities serving Somali communities. Operating in Nairobi Eastleigh (Kenya), Mogadishu (Somalia), Addis Ababa (Ethiopia), Djibouti (Djibouti), and Hargeisa (Somaliland), our platform connects travelers directly with elite hotels via WhatsApp in a commission-free environment.
