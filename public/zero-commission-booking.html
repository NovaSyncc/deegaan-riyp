‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do you make money if you don't charge commission?

We partner directly with hotels who pay us a small marketing fee (not passed to you) in exchange for bringing high-quality guests. Hotels prefer this model because they avoid the 15-25% commissions charged by traditional booking sites.

Q: Are the hotel rates really the same as if I called directly?

Yes, absolutely. In fact, many hotels offer slightly better rates through Deegaan-Riyo because they save on commission and want to encourage direct bookings. You can verify this by calling the hotel yourself.

Q: What if I need to cancel or change my booking?

Since you book directly with the hotel via WhatsApp, changes are handled by the hotel's standard policy - no additional fees from us. Many hotels are more flexible with direct bookings than with third-party reservations.

Q: Is my booking protected if something goes wrong?

Yes. You have a direct relationship with the hotel, which is actually stronger protection than booking through a third party. Plus, our team assists with any issues, and hotels prioritize resolving problems for direct guests.

Q: Can I earn points or miles for these bookings?

Yes! Since you're booking directly with the hotel, you earn full loyalty points, elite benefits, and can use your status for upgrades - benefits often not available through third-party sites.
